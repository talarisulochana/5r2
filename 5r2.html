A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, known as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned Indian scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts, earning him national recognition.
